# unfollowed.exe
A useful chrome extension that scrapes your Instagram follower and following list to find out who is not following you back. Does not require a login, meaning your account will not be at risk for botted behaviour.

## Installation
### Method 1 (Easiest)
- This method only works on Google Chrome
- Download unfollowed.exe from the [Chrome Webstore](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/unfollow3d/kdkgfkbcjjfohpedofakfeldkoejinpp)

### Method 2
- Download unfollowed.exe from Github
- Load it as an unpacked extension in Chrome at `chrome://extensions/`.

## Extension Instructions
1. Go to your Instagram profile on desktop
2. Open your follower list
3. Open the extension and press "Scan Followers"
4. When the end of the list is reached click "Stop"
5. Do the same for your following list
6. Open the extension and press "Compare"
